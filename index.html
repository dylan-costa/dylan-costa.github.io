Dylan Costa

About Me

My name is Dylan and I am currently a PhD candidate at the University of Connecticut (UConn) under the advisment of Alvaro Lozano-Robledo. My research interests are in Arithmetic Geometry. Specifically, I am looking into Galois Representations attached to Elliptic curves.

I graduated from Ithaca College in 2021 with a BA in Mathematics with Honors and a BM in Cello Performance. Outside of Mathematics, I enjoy playing cello and going to various music concerts. I play in a string quartet that puts on yearly chamber concerts during the summer in my hometown of Danbury, CT. Addtionally, I enjoy getting outside and playing different board games. If you want to contact me for any reason, my email is dylan.costa@uconn.edu.
